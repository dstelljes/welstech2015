Make it simple.

Bad

We profess our faith in Jesus Christ, the Bronze Serpent who was lifted up that we may partake of his body and blood and be free from the poison of sin, guaranteeing immortality with him in sinless bliss.

Better

We're a family of Christians eager to share God's unconditional love.

Keep it brief.

Bad

The Reverend Phillip H. Pastorson was born in Wall, South Dakota to Roger and Eunice Pastorson, a poor farm couple. After attending Lutheran schools for twelve years, Rev. Pastorson enrolled in Doctor Martin Luther College, where he earned his Bachelor of Arts, focusing primarily on Greek or whatever, I guess I don't really know what exactly those degrees are in. The point is, this is a super long paragraph that no one in their right mind is going to read.

Better

Phil Pastorson lives here in Tortilla Flat with his wife and two kids. Phil loves biking, fishing, and (most importantly!) serving the people of Our Savior's.

Call him at (480) 933-4863 or send him an email at pastor@oursaviors.net.
